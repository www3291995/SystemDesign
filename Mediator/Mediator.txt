Motivation

--Component may go in and out of a system at any time
	.Chat room participants
	.Players in an MMORPG
--It Makes no sense for them to have direct references to one another
	.Those references may go dead
--Solution: have then all refer to some central component that facilitates communication


Mediator
--A component that facilitates communication between other components without them
  necessarily being aware of each other or having direct(reference) access to each other.


Summary

--Create the mediator and have each object in the system refer to it
	--E.g., in a field
--Mediator engages in bidirectional communication with its connected components
--Mediator has functions the components can call
--Components have functions the mediator can call
--Event processing(e.g., Rx) libraries make communication easier to implement
